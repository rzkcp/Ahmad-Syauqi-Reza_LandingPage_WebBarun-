About Us

Welcome to Kopilot, your go-to coffee shop where every cup is an exploration. We proudly serve expertly crafted beverages made from the finest beans sourced globally. Enjoy a cozy atmosphere perfect for relaxing, catching up with friends, or diving into a good book. Pair your drink with our delicious pastries for a delightful experience. Join us at Kopilot and embark on your coffee journey today!

Kopilot Menu

Coffee Classics

  • Espresso: Bold and rich shot of espresso.
  • Americano: Espresso with hot water for a smooth finish.
  • Latte: Espresso with steamed milk and a light foam.
  • Cappuccino: Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam.
  • Macchiato: Espresso topped with a dollop of frothy milk.

Signature Brews

  • Kopilot Brew: Our special house blend, perfectly balanced.
  • Cold Brew: Smooth, slow-brewed coffee served over ice.
  • Nitro Coffee: Cold brew infused with nitrogen for a creamy texture.

Specialty Drinks

  • Caramel Cloud Latte: Creamy latte with caramel syrup and whipped cream.
  • Mocha Delight: Espresso with steamed milk and rich chocolate syrup.
  • Matcha Green Tea Latte: Smooth matcha blended with steamed milk.

Tea Selections

  • Chai Latte: Spiced tea with steamed milk and a touch of honey.
  • Herbal Infusion: A selection of calming herbal teas.
  • Iced Tea: Refreshing brewed tea, served chilled.

Pastries & Light Bites

  • Freshly Baked Croissants: Classic buttery croissants.
  • Assorted Muffins: Blueberry, chocolate chip, and banana nut.
  • Cookies: Chewy chocolate chip or oatmeal raisin.
  • Savory Scones: Cheese and chive or cranberry orange.

Light Meals

  • Avocado Toast: Smashed avocado on artisanal bread with toppings.
  • Breakfast Sandwich: Egg, cheese, and your choice of bacon or sausage.
  • Quinoa Salad: Fresh veggies, quinoa, and a lemon vinaigrette.

Seasonal Specials

  • Pumpkin Spice Latte (Fall): A cozy blend of espresso, steamed milk, and pumpkin spice.
  • Iced Strawberry Matcha (Summer): A refreshing blend of matcha and strawberry puree.
